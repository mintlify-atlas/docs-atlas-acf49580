## Message Organization

### Channels and Topics

Every message in Zulip belongs to a **channel** (like #general or #engineering) and has a **topic** (like "Bug #1234" or "Q1 Planning"). This dual-layer organization means:

- Multiple conversations can happen simultaneously in the same channel
- You can catch up on specific topics without reading everything
- Conversations can span hours or days without getting lost
- Search and reference past discussions easily

<Tip>
  **Topic-based threading** is Zulip's superpower. It combines the organization of email with the immediacy of chat.
</Tip>

### Smart Inbox

The inbox view shows all your unread messages organized by channel and topic:

- See at a glance which conversations need your attention
- Mark entire topics as read with one click
- Filter by direct messages, mentions, or channels
- Zero inbox pressure - come back to conversations when convenient

### Combined Feed

View all messages from unmuted channels and topics in one continuous feed, or narrow to specific channels, topics, or search queries.

### Mentions and Notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Personal mentions">
    Use `@username` to notify specific users. They'll see a highlight and get a notification based on their settings.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Group mentions">
    Notify everyone in a channel with `@**all**` or specific user groups with `@**group-name**`.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Topic follows">
    Follow specific topics to get notifications for all messages, even without being mentioned.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wildcard mentions">
    Use `@**channel**` to notify all subscribers to a channel (requires permissions).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Use wildcard mentions sparingly - they notify everyone subscribed to the channel.
</Warning>

## Search and Navigation

### Advanced Search

Zulip's search is designed for power users:

```
stream:general topic:"feature requests" is:starred
```

Search operators include:
- `stream:name` - Search within a specific channel
- `topic:name` - Search within a topic
- `sender:user` - Messages from a specific user
- `is:starred`, `is:mentioned`, `is:private` - Filter by message type
- `has:link`, `has:image`, `has:attachment` - Filter by content
- `near:12345` - Show context around message ID

### Narrowing

Narrowing focuses your view on specific conversations:

- Click a channel to see all messages in that channel
- Click a topic to see just that conversation
- Use keyboard shortcuts to narrow efficiently
- Share narrow URLs to link teammates to specific conversations

<Info>
  Narrow URLs preserve the exact context, making it easy to reference past discussions.
</Info>

### Keyboard Shortcuts

Power through your workflow with keyboard shortcuts:

- `C` - Compose a new channel message
- `X` - Start a new topic
- `R` - Reply to the selected message
- `S` - Narrow to a topic
- `/` - Search messages
- `N` / `P` - Next/previous unread message
- `?` - Show all keyboard shortcuts

Create custom integrations easily:

